Project Phoenix - iOS Beta 1

Community-built app to keep Vitruvian Trainer machines working after company closure.

CORE FEATURES:
- BLE connection to V-Form Trainer and Trainer+ machines
- 572 exercises with video demonstrations
- Real-time workout metrics (load, position, velocity, power)

WORKOUT MODES:
- Old School - Standard resistance training
- Pump - High-rep pump mode
- TUT / TUT Beast - Time Under Tension modes
- Eccentric Only - Lowering phase focus
- Echo - Adaptive mode (Hard/Harder/Hardest/Epic levels, 0-150% eccentric load)

TRAINING FEATURES:
- Just Lift - Quick start with auto weight detection
- Custom Routines with superset support
- Training Cycles - Rolling workout schedules
- AMRAP (As Many Reps As Possible) sets
- Warmup rep tracking
- Rest timers between sets

TRACKING:
- Personal Record detection with 1RM calculations
- Workout history and analytics
- Badge achievements and streak tracking

Early beta - requires physical Vitruvian Trainer.
